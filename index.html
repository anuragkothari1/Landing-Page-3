Peel Formulas

At The Formula, we offer chemical peels as an effective and safe solution for achieving brighter, smoother, and more youthful-looking skin. Chemical peels are a cosmetic treatment that utilizes a specialized solution to the skin to exfoliate and remove the top layer of dead skin cells. This process encourages the regeneration of new skin cells, revealing a smoother, more radiant complexion. Whether you're looking to reduce the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles, improve acne scars or hyperpigmentation, or refresh your skin, our expert aestheticians are here to help. Book a chemical peel appointment online today to get started!

Laser Formulas

At The Formula, we offer laser resurfacing and rejuvenation treatments using the state-of-the-art Lutronic ULTRA™ laser device. Laser resurfacing is a non-surgical cosmetic treatment that uses laser energy to target and remove damaged skin cells, encouraging the growth of new, healthy cells. With the Lutronic ULTRA™ device, we can provide safe and effective treatments customized to meet your unique skin needs. If you’re interested in rejuvenating your skin and achieving a more youthful complexion, book an appointment online today to learn more about our laser resurfacing and rejuvenation treatments at The Formula.

Thread Lift

We understand the importance of healthy, youthful-looking skin, and our team at The Formula is dedicated to helping you achieve your best skin with minimal downtime. Our experienced providers offer PDO thread lifts, a safe and effective treatment that uses polydioxanone sutures to lift and tighten the skin on the face, neck, and body. These sutures are commonly used in surgical procedures, but PDO thread lifts offer the same benefits without the risks associated with surgery. Let us help you achieve natural-looking results that will leave you feeling confident and beautiful.

Neurotoxins

At The Formula, we're dedicated to helping you achieve healthy, youthful skin with the help of neurotoxins. Neurotoxins are a safe, non-invasive, and effective way to smooth the appearance of lines, wrinkles, and other signs of aging. Our skilled injectors use only the highest quality neurotoxins, including Botox, Daxxify, Dysport, & Xeomin, to ensure safe and effective results that look natural and beautiful. Whether you're interested in reducing the appearance of wrinkles or controlling excessive sweating, our team is ready to help you achieve your best skin.

Dermal Fillers

At The Formula, we offer a range of advanced skincare treatments, including dermal fillers, to help you achieve your aesthetic goals. Dermal fillers are an effective and non-invasive way to address various concerns, from fine lines and wrinkles to loss of volume and definition.

Aquagold
